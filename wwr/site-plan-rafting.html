Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to bring in new customers with our new website, which will also make advertising and bookings easier. It will provide information about available white water rafting experiences through our company White Water Adventures. Access to this information will generate interest and help customers customize their rafting experience. Our website will also bring awareness of rafting to those who have not been exposed to this recreational activity. Furthermore, it will educate potential customers on the history of white water rafting, the tools needed for the activity, the skills fostered by rafting, and recommendations on how to get started. Customers will be able to reserve dates, equipment, hire a river guide, recommendations for hotels or camping sites in the area, and customize their white water rafting experience. There will be pictures and videos of people participating in rafting, as well as testimonials from previous customers. Finally, the website will contact information to White Water Adventures for general information, reservations, and customer service. By increasing awareness of our business and creating ease of access, we increase profits.

Audience

Adventure Enthusiasts: White water rafting appeals to individuals who enjoy challenging and adrenaline-pumping activities. Outdoor and Nature Lovers: People who appreciate the beauty of nature and the outdoors are often attracted to white water rafting. Rafting trips often take place in scenic and natural environments, offering participants a unique way to connect with nature while enjoying an active adventure. Groups and Team-Building Events: White water rafting is a popular choice for group activities and team-building events. Companies, organizations, and social groups often organize rafting trips as a way to foster teamwork, communication, and camaraderie among participants. Fitness Enthusiasts: Rafting requires physical effort and teamwork. Individuals who enjoy staying active and participating in fitness-oriented activities may find white water rafting to be a fulfilling and engaging outdoor pursuit. Travelers and Tourists: Tourists and travelers looking for unique and memorable experiences often include white water rafting in their adventure itineraries. Rafting destinations attract people seeking a combination of excitement and exploration in different regions. Young Adults and Millennials: The younger demographic, including young adults and millennials, is often drawn to adventurous and experiential activities. White water rafting aligns with the desire for unique and Instagram-worthy experiences. Families and Recreational Groups: White water rafting companies often cater to families and recreational groups. Some rivers have sections with milder rapids suitable for beginners and children, making it a family-friendly activity. Water Sports Enthusiasts: Individuals who have an interest in water sports, such as kayaking or canoeing, may also be drawn to white water rafting. Rafting provides a dynamic and social water-based experience.
